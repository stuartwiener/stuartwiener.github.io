I’m a designer, creator and speaker living and working in Johannesburg, South Africa. I work as a Product Design Director (for mobile banking) at Absa Group.

Design, eh?

To design is to consciously and intuitively shape any thing. Everything is designed—seriously, everything is designed—and that means you are a designer.

Curiosity killed the cat, but never a designer. And anyway, the cat was happy, thrilled by discovery.

People are complex, surprising, wonderful and different to you and I.

There’s a world brimming with gold if you listen and pay careful attention.

New and different is relatively easy. Real progress takes some serious work.

Be brave. Stop reading this and start.
