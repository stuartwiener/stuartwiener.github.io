I’m a designer living and working in Johannesburg, South Africa. I work as a Product Design Director (for mobile banking) at Barclays Africa Group Limited.

The only design advice I can give you (unless we have a coffee together sometime) is...

  • Everything—seriously, everything—is designed.
  • Why? is probably the most useful question to ask.
  • Curiosity is a prerequisite for making something great.
  • Humans are complex, unpredictable and wonderful.
  • You can learn a lot by listening and paying attention.
  • You have to make something wrong before you can make it right.
  • It’s easy to make something not bad. You must be brave to make it great.
  • Stop reading this and go play outside.
