I’m a designer living and working in Johannesburg, South Africa. I work as a Product Design Director (for mobile banking) at Barclays Africa.

To design is to consciously and intuitively shape any thing. Everything is designed—seriously, everything—and you are a designer.

Curiosity killed the cat, but there is no record of it ever killing a designer.

People are complex, surprising, wonderful and different to you and I.

There’s a world brimming with gold if you listen and pay careful attention.

New and different is relatively easy. Actual progress takes some serious work.

Be brave. Stop reading this and start.
